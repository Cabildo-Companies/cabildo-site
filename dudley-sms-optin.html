Dudley SMS Opt-In

Program: Dudley by Cabildo Companies · Effective June 30, 2026

Dudley is a conversational SMS concierge operated by Cabildo Companies LLC for its consumer products (Cour at mycour.app, Portage, and Routabout). Users text Dudley questions about their own saved content — family archive, memories, places, and travel plans — and Dudley replies. Traffic is user-initiated and conversational.

This page documents exactly how end users opt in to Dudley SMS, including a demonstration of the in-app opt-in screen, which normally appears behind an account login.

Path 1 — Consumer-initiated (user texts first)

The user saves Dudley's contact card from inside the app and sends the first message themselves. Sending that first message constitutes opt-in. Dudley's first reply always identifies the program and includes opt-out and help instructions:

Hi, it's Dudley — your concierge at Cour. You're all set. Text me anytime about your people, places, and memories. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.

Path 2 — In-app request (web form behind login)

Inside the Cour app, the user enters their phone number and checks an unchecked consent box to request an SMS introduction from Dudley. Consent is recorded with a timestamp, the source URL, and the exact version of the consent language shown.
