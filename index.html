Meet Dudley

Your guide to the
places that matter.

Dudley is an AI travel guide that helps families discover what's worth seeing, plan trips worth taking, and hold onto the ones they've already been on.

What Dudley does

Knows the place

Tell Dudley where you're going. He'll tell you what's actually worth your time — the stops that make a trip, not the ones that fill a brochure.

Remembers the trip

Every trip you take becomes part of your story. Dudley helps you hold onto the details before they fade — the names, the places, the moments.

Learns what you care about

Family travel. History. Great food. Dudley pays attention to what matters to you, and gets better at it over time.

Talk to Dudley however you want

Dudley meets you where you are — in the app, by text, or wherever you need a knowledgeable friend who has actually been paying attention.

  • Text him a destination — he'll send back what's worth seeing
  • Ask him what to stop for on the drive
  • Let him help you remember a trip while it's still fresh
  • Find out what families like yours loved about a place
