Privacy Policy

Effective June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy covers Cabildo Companies and all products and services operated under the Cabildo family, including Dudley, Portage, Cour, and Routabout. When you use any Cabildo product or communicate with Dudley, this policy applies.

What we collect

When you use our products or contact Dudley, we may collect:

  • Your phone number (when you text Dudley or link your number to an account)
  • Your email address (when you create an account or contact us)
  • Information you share in conversations with Dudley, including trip plans, places, and preferences
  • Usage data about how you interact with our products
  • Device and browser information

How we use it

We use this information to:

  • Provide and improve Dudley and other Cabildo products
  • Send text messages or emails you have requested or consented to receive
  • Personalize your experience over time
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

SMS and text messaging

If you consent to receive text messages from Dudley, we will use your phone number to send you messages related to trip planning, travel recommendations, and other features you have requested. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out of SMS messages at any time, reply STOP to any message from Dudley. To get help, reply HELP. See our SMS Terms for full details.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third-party advertising networks or data brokers.

We may share information with service providers who help us operate our products (such as cloud infrastructure, messaging services, and analytics), under contracts that restrict their use to providing those services.

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect the safety of our users or the public.

Your choices

  • SMS opt-out: Reply STOP to any text from Dudley
  • Account deletion: Contact us at privacy@cabildocompanies.com
  • Data requests: Email us to request a copy of your data or ask what we have

SMS/text messaging and mobile information

When you opt in to receive text messages from Dudley, we collect your phone number, your opt-in consent (with a timestamp and the consent language you were shown), and the content of your text conversations with Dudley, solely to provide the service you requested.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with, or sold to, any third parties for any purpose.

You can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. Reply HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. See our SMS Terms for full details.

Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Consent records are retained permanently as required for legal compliance. You may request deletion of other personal data by contacting us.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No system is perfectly secure; if you have concerns, contact us at privacy@cabildocompanies.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of our products after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
